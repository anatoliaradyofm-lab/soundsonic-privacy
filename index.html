Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

SoundSonic ("we", "our", or "us") is a music discovery application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

SoundSonic does not require account registration. We do not collect, store, or process:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Photos, videos, or media files from your device
  • Location data
  • Microphone or camera input
  • Contacts or calendar data

2. Automatically Collected Information

The app may collect limited technical information necessary for operation:

  • Device identifiers — used by our advertising partner (Google AdMob) to serve relevant ads.
  • App usage data — anonymized crash reports and performance metrics to improve stability.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

SoundSonic displays ads provided by Google AdMob. Google may use an advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID) on your device to show personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID

Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Music Streaming

Music content is streamed from the Audius decentralized music platform. SoundSonic does not store or cache audio files on our servers. Audius's privacy policy governs data processed through their platform.

5. Internet Access

The app requires internet access solely to stream music and load content. No personal data is transmitted to our servers during this process.

6. Children's Privacy

SoundSonic is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
