3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR – EU/EEA Users)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, we process data under the following legal bases as defined by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

Processing Activity Legal Basis Article
Serving personalized ads via AdMob Consent Art. 6(1)(a)
Serving non-personalized ads Legitimate Interest Art. 6(1)(f)
App stability monitoring (anonymized) Legitimate Interest Art. 6(1)(f)
Music streaming (IP-based requests) Performance of service Art. 6(1)(b)

You may withdraw consent for personalized advertising at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing based on consent before withdrawal.

4. How We Use Your Data

Data is used exclusively for:

  • Displaying advertisements to support the free service
  • Streaming music content to your device
  • Improving app performance through anonymized diagnostics
  • Personalizing the home feed based on your device's region (country code derived from device locale — not stored on our servers)

We do not use your data for profiling, automated decision-making, or any purpose other than those listed above.

5. Advertising – Google AdMob

SoundSonic uses Google AdMob to display ads. Google may use your Android Advertising ID (AAID) to show personalized advertisements based on your interests.

To opt out of personalized ads:

  • Android 12+: Settings → Privacy → Ads → Delete advertising ID
  • Android <12: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

Even if you opt out, you will still see non-personalized ads. Google's data practices are governed by the Google Privacy Policy.

6. Music Streaming – Audius

Music content is streamed from the Audius decentralized network. When you stream a track, your IP address may be processed by Audius nodes to deliver the audio. We do not store, log, or process this data ourselves. Please review the Audius Privacy Policy for details.

7. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Google AdMob — for ad delivery (as described above)
  • Audius — for music streaming (IP address only)
  • Law enforcement — only when required by applicable law or valid legal process

8. International Data Transfers

Our services involve third parties (Google, Audius) that may process data in the United States and other countries outside the EU/EEA. These transfers are carried out under:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Google's Data Processing Terms, which include appropriate safeguards

By using SoundSonic, you acknowledge that your data may be transferred internationally in accordance with the safeguards described above.

9. Data Retention

SoundSonic itself does not store personal data on our servers. Data retention periods for third-party processors:

  • Google AdMob: Advertising ID data retained per Google's policy (typically up to 18 months for analytics purposes)
  • Anonymized crash reports: Retained for up to 90 days
  • Audius streaming logs: Governed by Audius's retention policy

Local app data (e.g., listening history stored on your device) remains on your device and can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

10. Your Rights

EU/EEA Residents (GDPR)

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access (Art. 15) — Request a copy of data held about you
  • Right to Rectification (Art. 16) — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure / "Right to be Forgotten" (Art. 17) — Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Restriction of Processing (Art. 18) — Request limitation of how your data is used
  • Right to Data Portability (Art. 20) — Receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Right to Object (Art. 21) — Object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to Withdraw Consent (Art. 7(3)) — Withdraw consent for personalized ads at any time
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint — You may file a complaint with your local Data Protection Authority (DPA)

Since we do not collect personal data directly, most requests will be directed to the relevant third party (e.g., Google). To exercise your rights, contact us at anatoliaradyofm@gmail.com.

UK Residents (UK GDPR)

Your rights under the UK GDPR are equivalent to those listed above. You may lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

Turkish Residents (KVKK)

Under Turkey's Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK No. 6698), you have the right to learn whether your personal data is being processed, request information about it, and object to processing. Contact us at the email above to exercise these rights.

11. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

SoundSonic is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EU/EEA under GDPR). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at anatoliaradyofm@gmail.com and we will take steps to delete such information.

12. California Residents (CCPA / CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • Right to Know — Categories of personal information collected and purposes of use
  • Right to Delete — Request deletion of personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out of Sale or Sharing — We do not sell or share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising
  • Right to Non-Discrimination — We will not discriminate against you for exercising your CCPA rights
  • Right to Correct — Request correction of inaccurate personal information
  • Right to Limit Use of Sensitive Personal Information — Not applicable as we do not collect sensitive personal information

Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information: SoundSonic does not sell personal information. The Android Advertising ID used by Google AdMob may constitute "sharing" under CCPA. You can opt out by disabling the Advertising ID in your device settings.

To exercise your California rights, contact: anatoliaradyofm@gmail.com
